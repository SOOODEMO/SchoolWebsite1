Tie Dye

Tie Dyes happen at the school and it's very creative and when your finished your tie dye shirt, you can keep it! You can Tie Dye a T-Shirt, Pants, Shorts, Gowns, or normal shirts. Don't get splashy when making you Tie Dye!

Fun Fair

The Fun Fairs are a part of the Niagra Islamic School were kids go on Trampolines, play Games, and have fun. This Activity is open for any kid in the neighborhood to go see, but in the school Kindergartens can play on the Fun Fairs.
